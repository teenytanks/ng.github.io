Units

Tank type units cannot move or shoot through terrain. Infantry units can move and aim through terrain.
Only two units of one type can be drafted.

  • Range: 4

Scout

  • Damage: 2
  • Range: 2
  • Movement: 6
  • Health: 10

Saboteur
Infantry

  • Damage: 0
  • Range: 5
  • Movement: 4
  • Health: 10
  • Chance: 1d6 > 3
Abilities
  • On successful attack, take control of target enemy unit for this turn. Enemy unit cannot attack itself.
  • Player loses if saboteur is their last unit
