Combat

A unit rolls a dice each time it performs an attack to check for a successful hit. If the attack is unsuccesful, the player who rolled the attack gains 1 calibration.

For each unit of calibration, the next attack roll said player performs will gain a 1/6 chance of succeeding. For example, if player 1 rolls an attack with a basic tank, but misses, they gain 1 calibration. Even if they use a different unit next turn, the attack roll for said unit will gain the benefits of calibration.

All calibration belonging to the rolling player is used up on a successful hit.
